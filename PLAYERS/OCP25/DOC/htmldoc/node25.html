Phase graphs

The last graphical function is starts with <b>. You can toggle between four modes which refer exactly to those in the oscilloscope mode. This function displays the currently played samples in a phase graph. One full wave of the sample is drawn over the complete angle of a circle. The louder the sample the greater the radius of the circle. A sine sample would respond to a normal circle.
